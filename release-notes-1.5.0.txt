Co je nového v 1.5.0:

• Organizátor má plnou kontrolu nad všemi účastníky — mazání, úprava rolí, RSVP i u reálných uživatelů

<en-US>
What's new in 1.5.0:

• Organizer has full control over all participants — delete, edit roles, RSVP for real users too
• New edit participant dialog — change name (guests) and custom role (everyone)
• Proxy voting for any participant — not just guests, but also users who don't want to use the app
• Delete icon now visible directly in the participant list
• Fixed incorrect "Role updated" message when removing a participant
</en-US>
