App name:
Better Mingle – Group Events

Short description (max 80 chars):
Organize events, split expenses, vote and plan carpools – all in one place.

Long description (max 4000 chars):
Better Mingle is an app for organizing group events – whether it's a trip with friends, a corporate team-building, a birthday party, or a weekend getaway.

Create an event, invite friends with a single link, and plan everything together in one place.

🎯 What you can do:

• Create events – set a name, date, location, and description. Add the modules you need.
• Split expenses – add expenses, split them among participants, and let the app calculate who owes whom.
• Voting – create polls and decide together. Results in real time.
• Carpooling – offer seats in your car or join a ride. A simple overview of who's riding with whom.
• Rooms – organize accommodation. Create rooms, set capacity, and let participants assign themselves.
• Packing list – a shared list of things to bring. Everyone can see what's already covered.
• Tasks – divide work among participants. Assign tasks and track what's done.
• Schedule – plan the event's timeline from morning to evening.
• Chat – communicate with participants directly in the app.
• Activity feed – an overview of everything happening at the event.
• PIN protection – secure your event with a PIN to keep uninvited guests out.

📱 Who is Better Mingle for?

• A group of friends planning a trip or vacation
• Birthday party and event organizers
• Corporate teams on a team-building
• Roommates sharing expenses
• Anyone who wants to keep planning under control
